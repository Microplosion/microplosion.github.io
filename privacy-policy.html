Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 9, 2025

Microplosion Labs develops privacy-focused Android apps such as Cache-Plosion (Total Cache Cleaner Pro). This Privacy Policy explains how our apps handle data, permissions, and advertising.

1. Information Collection

Microplosion Labs does not collect, store, or transmit personal or sensitive user data on its own servers. We do not ask for names, emails, contacts, messages, photos, or any personally identifiable information inside the app.

All cleaning actions occur entirely on your device and under your direct control. We do not maintain remote logs or server-side usage profiles. Limited device-level information may be processed by third-party advertising partners as described in Section 3.

2. Permissions Used

  • Accessibility Service – used only to automate the “Clear cache” button inside app settings while performing cleaning steps that the user initiates. This service does not read keystrokes, passwords, messages, screen contents, or personal data. No information accessed through this service is stored or transmitted.
  • Usage Access – used only to help determine which apps are installed so the user can choose which app caches to clean. No app usage history or activity data is transmitted off the device.
  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES – used strictly to show the full list of installed apps so users can select which app caches to clean. This permission is not used for analytics, advertising, tracking, profiling, or any unrelated purpose. No package information is uploaded or shared.
  • Internet Access – used only to load ads (when applicable) and check for optional app updates. The app does not inspect your browsing activity or network traffic.

Accessibility permission is required only to automate cleaning steps you start; no personal data is collected or shared by Microplosion Labs.

3. Advertising and Analytics

Some versions of the app may show third-party ads (e.g., Google AdMob). Advertising partners may process limited device information such as:

  • Android Advertising ID (AAID)
  • Approximate region or country
  • Device model, OS version, language
  • IP address (for fraud prevention and network communication)

This information is used to deliver, measure, and improve ads. Microplosion Labs does not have access to raw ad identifiers in a personally identifiable way.

Users may disable personalized ads in Android’s system settings. We do not include analytics SDKs that collect personal or sensitive user data.

4. Data Sharing

Microplosion Labs does not sell, rent, or trade user data. We do not upload your app list, cache data, or any on-device information to our servers.

Third-party advertising networks may receive basic, non-personal device information as part of normal ad delivery. No personal data is shared.

Any data processed by third-party services is subject to their own privacy policies.

5. Security Practices

We follow Android’s built-in permission and security model. The app cannot access your photos, messages, files, or contacts. All cache-cleaning functions run locally on your device. We do not store logs of your cleaning activity.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If a child provides information, please contact us so we can remove it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, the “Effective Date” will be updated. Continued app use after changes indicates acceptance.
