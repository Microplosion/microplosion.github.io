Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 9, 2025

Microplosion Labs develops Android privacy and performance apps such as Cache-Plosion (Total Cache Cleaner Pro). This Privacy Policy explains how our apps handle data, permissions, and advertising.

1. Information Collection

Our apps do not collect, store, or share any personal or sensitive information. We do not collect names, emails, or device identifiers.

2. Permissions Used

  • Accessibility Service – used only to automate cache cleaning steps that the user initiates. This permission is required to tap system buttons on the user's behalf for faster cleaning. No screen content, keystrokes, or personal data are stored, shared, or transmitted outside the device.
  • Usage Access – used to identify installed apps and manage their cache. No external data transfer occurs.
  • Internet Access – used solely to load ads or check for app updates.

Requires Accessibility permission to automate cache cleaning — no personal data is collected or shared.

3. Advertising and Analytics

Some apps show third-party ads (e.g., Google AdMob). These may use your device’s advertising ID to deliver relevant content. You can opt out of personalized ads in your Android settings.

No analytics SDKs are used that collect personally identifiable data.
