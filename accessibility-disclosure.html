Accessibility Service Disclosure

App name: Cache-Plosion (Total Cache Cleaner Pro)

Developer: Microplosion Labs

Purpose of the Accessibility Service

The Accessibility Service is used solely to automate cache-clearing actions within the Android Settings app. It helps users by navigating to each selected app’s storage page and performing the “Clear cache” action on their behalf — exactly as if the user tapped the button manually. All actions are initiated by the user and limited only to cache cleaning.

Data Handling & Privacy

  • The service does not read or collect screen content, text, keystrokes, passwords, messages, or any personal data.
  • No input data, personal data, or sensitive information is captured, logged, stored, or transmitted.
  • All automation and interactions occur entirely on the user’s device and never leave the device.
  • The service performs no background monitoring or tracking.

User Consent & Control

The user must manually enable the Accessibility Service from Android’s system settings before it can operate. The service runs only while the user has explicitly granted permission and may be disabled at any time in the device’s Accessibility settings.

Compliance & Transparency

This Accessibility Service complies with Google Play’s Accessibility Policy . It exists exclusively to support the core functionality of Cache-Plosion: helping users clear cached data faster.

The service does not alter other device settings, perform actions outside the stated purpose, or access unrelated apps or data.
