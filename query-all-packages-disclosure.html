QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES Permission Disclosure

App name: Cache-Plosion (Total Cache Cleaner Pro)

Developer: Microplosion Labs

Why This Permission Is Required

Cache-Plosion uses the Android permission QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES for a single, core purpose:

To display the full list of installed apps so the user can choose which app caches to clean.

Cache cleaning requires the ability to show installed applications, navigate to their settings page, and automate the “Clear cache” button when the user initiates the action. Without knowing which apps are installed, this functionality is not possible.

How the Permission Is Used

  • To load a complete list of installed apps (both user and system apps).
  • To display storage and cache usage for apps you select.
  • To allow you to choose which apps to clean during a user-initiated cleaning session.

Cache-Plosion does not use this permission for anything else.

What the Permission Is Not Used For

This permission is not used for:

  • Advertising targeting
  • Analytics or data collection
  • Tracking users or devices
  • Profiling or monitoring behavior
  • Reading data from other apps
  • Accessing private files, messages, photos, or media

The app does not upload your installed app list or device information to Microplosion Labs servers. All processing happens locally on your device.

Data Handling & Privacy

  • No app data, lists, or identifiers are transmitted off the device.
  • No analytics SDKs collect information about which apps you have installed.
  • No personal or sensitive data is accessed, stored, or shared.
  • The permission is used only in combination with user-initiated cache cleaning.

For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

Google Play Policy Compliance

This permission is requested strictly under Google Play’s “Core Functionality” requirement for apps that need visibility of installed apps. Cache-Plosion cannot perform its primary function (cache cleaning) without showing the user a complete list of apps.
