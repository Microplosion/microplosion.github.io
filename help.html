Need help with Cache-Plosion?

This page explains how Cache-Plosion works, why it needs certain permissions, and how to fix the most common issues in just a few taps.

  • Tap once · Clean many apps — Cache-Plosion opens each app’s system settings and taps “Clear cache” for you.
  • No root required — it uses official Android screens like App info → Storage & cache → Clear cache.
  • Privacy-first — Cache-Plosion does not read your messages, photos, or personal content.
  • Ad-free options — unlock lifetime ad-free or earn ad-free time from referrals (if enabled in your version).
Quick start:
1. Open Cache-Plosion
2. Follow the permission checklist
3. Tap Scan & Clean / Clear all cache
4. Watch the apps open one by one while Cache-Plosion taps “Clear cache” automatically

1. Getting started

Setup guide

Once you install Cache-Plosion on your Android device, follow these steps so the cleaner can run smoothly:

  1. Open Cache-Plosion from your app drawer or home screen.
  2. On the first screen, review the permissions checklist and tap each item to enable it.
  3. After permissions are granted, you’ll see the dashboard with your storage summary and cleaning options.
  4. Tap Scan or Clear all cache (depending on your version) to start the automated clean.
  5. Let Cache-Plosion drive: you’ll see Android’s App info and Storage & cache screens as the app taps “Clear cache” for each selected app.
Tip: Keep your phone unlocked and don’t tap other buttons while Cache-Plosion is cleaning. If you need to stop, use the on-screen pause/stop controls provided by the app (if shown).

2. Why Cache-Plosion needs these permissions

Transparency

Cache-Plosion uses only the permissions it needs to find apps and trigger the official Clear cache buttons in Android’s own settings. It does not read your conversations, photos, or private content.

Required
Accessibility Service

Used to tap buttons like “Storage & cache” and “Clear cache” on your behalf on the official Android settings screens.

  • Go to Settings → Accessibility → Installed services.
  • Find Cache-Plosion and turn it On.
  • Confirm the prompt to allow it to perform these actions.
Required
Usage access / App usage

Used to detect which apps are installed and how much cache they are using (where available), so Cache-Plosion knows which apps to open in Settings.

  • Go to Settings → Apps → Special access → Usage access.
  • Tap Cache-Plosion and enable Allow usage access.
Optional
Notifications

Used to show progress, results, and tips (for example, how much storage was freed after a clean).

If you turn notifications off, the cleaner will still work, but you might not see helpful alerts.

Optional
Network / Internet

Used only for:

  • Displaying ads (free version)
  • Checking purchases and referral rewards (ad-free options)
  • Basic anonymous analytics or update checks (if enabled in your version)

Cache-Plosion is designed to be privacy-first and does not sell your personal data.

Reminder: If any permission is turned off later, the automation may stop working. You can always reopen Cache-Plosion to re-run the permission checklist.

3. How the cleaning process works

Behind the scenes

Cache-Plosion doesn’t hack or modify your system. It simply speeds up what you could do manually: opening each app’s settings and tapping “Clear cache”.

  1. You tap Clear all cache (or similar) inside Cache-Plosion.
  2. The app selects a list of installed apps that are safe to cache-clean.
  3. For each app, Cache-Plosion opens that app’s App info screen.
  4. Using Accessibility, it taps “Storage & cache” and then “Clear cache”.
  5. When the sequence is done, you see a summary of how much space was freed.
Important: Cache-Plosion does not tap “Clear storage” or “Clear data” for your apps. It focuses on cache, so your logins and app data stay safe.

4. Ads, ad-free unlock, and referrals

Monetization & rewards

Cache-Plosion may show ads in the free version to help support development. In some versions, you’ll see options for lifetime ad-free or referral-based ad-free time.

Lifetime ad-free unlock (one-time purchase)

If your version of Cache-Plosion offers a one-time unlock, you can pay once to remove ads permanently.

  • Open Cache-Plosion and go to Settings or the Go Ad-Free section.
  • Tap Unlock lifetime ad-free.
  • Confirm the purchase with your Google Play account.
  • Once confirmed, ads are removed for that Google account on this app.

Purchases are handled securely by Google Play Billing. Cache-Plosion does not store your card details.

Referral rewards (earn ad-free time)

You may see a referral feature where sharing Cache-Plosion with friends gives you ad-free time as a reward (for example, 2 weeks ad-free per successful referral).

  • Open Cache-Plosion and find the Invite friends or Referral option.
  • Share your unique link with friends.
  • When a friend installs and opens the app from your link, your ad-free timer increases.

Exact reward amounts and rules may vary by version or region, but the idea is simple: the more friends you help, the less ads you see.

Do ads affect cleaning performance?

No. Ads do not change how Cache-Plosion cleans. They may appear:

  • On the dashboard
  • Before or after a cleaning session

The cleaning engine itself stays the same in both the free and ad-free versions.

5. Frequently asked questions

FAQ
Why do I still see cache after cleaning?

Some apps quickly rebuild their cache the next time you open them. This is normal Android behavior and not a problem with Cache-Plosion. The cleaner did clear the cache — it’s just being rebuilt as the app runs.

Does Cache-Plosion delete my photos, messages, or accounts?

No. Cache-Plosion focuses on temporary cache files. It does not delete your photos, videos, contacts, SMS, or login accounts. It does not tap “Clear storage” or “Clear data”.

The app stopped cleaning automatically. What happened?

Most of the time this means a permission was turned off by the system or a phone update.

  • Reopen Cache-Plosion and check the permission checklist.
  • Make sure Accessibility and Usage access are still enabled.
  • If you use a battery saver, allow Cache-Plosion to run without being put to sleep.
Is Cache-Plosion safe to use?

Cache-Plosion uses official Android screens and buttons. It does not perform hidden system modifications or require root. You always see what the app is doing as it opens settings and taps “Clear cache”.

Does Cache-Plosion work on iPhone (iOS)?

Cache-Plosion is currently designed for Android devices only. iOS does not allow third-party apps to clear other apps’ cache in the same way.

6. Quick troubleshooting checklist

Fix common issues

If something isn’t working, run through these quick checks:

  1. Permissions: Reopen Cache-Plosion and confirm all required permissions are enabled.
  2. Accessibility reset: Turn the Accessibility service off and back on for Cache-Plosion.
  3. Reboot: Restart your phone and try a clean again.
  4. Update: Make sure you’re using the latest version from Google Play.
  5. Battery saver: Allow Cache-Plosion to run without aggressive background limits.
If you still have issues after these steps, please contact support with your device model, Android version, and a short description of what happens. This helps us improve Cache-Plosion for everyone.
